About

Main Title About Karbo

Karbo does not have centralized management or issuer and ensures privacy and anonymity of transactions without intermediaries and regulators.

Privacy Matters

The anonymity of payments without the ability to track them, resistance to the blockchain analysis is provided by technology CryptoNote.

Liberty is Your Full Control

Only you control your money. No one can confiscate them, seize them, deny your access, or restrict your freedom to dispose of your money.

No Intermediaries and Regulators

Transactions take place through a decentralized system. Emissions system operates according to a predefined mathematical algorithm without central control bodies.

Openness and Accessibility

Karbo is an open and decentralized network. Everyone is welcomed to contribute, participate in mining, development and governance.

Security and Reliability

The network, transactions, your account, and your coins are cryptographically protected (encrypted).

All Coins are Equal

Fungibility means all coins have equal value and are interchangeable because it is impossible to trace their history.
